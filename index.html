Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide such as your name, email address, phone number, and NEET rank when you register or contact us for counselling services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide personalised counselling guidance, communicate updates about your admission process, and improve our services. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data including SSL encryption and secure database storage via Supabase.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and authenticate sessions. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

For any privacy-related queries, please reach out to us at info@dcneet.com or call us at +91 98765 43210.
